How it works

Five steps from design file to engineering action.

1. Upload board design files
Single-board review, project reviews, CAM packages, and saved workspaces.
2. Silicore parses and evaluates design risk
Format-aware parsing across KiCad, Gerber/CAM, and expanding import paths.
3. Atlas Intelligence identifies concerns
Findings are clustered, prioritized, and framed as engineering decisions.
4. Risk scoring and comparisons surface priorities
Use board-level and workspace-level posture to guide the next review loop.
5. Automations notify teams and trigger follow-up
Reports, validation, alerts, and release workflow can all move from the same platform.
From analysis to action
