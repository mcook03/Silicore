What Silicore does

A single layer of intelligence across layout, checks, and review workflow.

Built for teams that ship hardware on aggressive timelines: fewer hand-wavy reviews, more traceable rationale tied to the artifacts you already produce.

01 — Ingestion

Deep PCB understanding, not keyword heuristics

Parse KiCad, Gerber, and CAM bundles into a consistent model of nets, copper, stackup assumptions, and fabrication posture—so downstream scoring reflects the board you actually built.

02 — Analysis

Cross-domain risk with engineering-grade framing

Evaluate integrity, thermal, EMI, manufacturability, and safety signals in one pass. Every issue is positioned with severity, mechanism, and what to verify on the next spin.

03 — Action

From detection to the next review step

Compare revisions, track score movement across projects, and route critical deltas into summaries your leads can sign off on—without rebuilding context from scratch each time.
